This listening test is to measure the relationship between the type of distortion applied to guitar sounds and the sense of heaviness perceived by the listener.

You will answer a series of questions comparing audio clips of guitar riffs with different distortions and EQs applied, and indicate which one of each pair you think sounds heavier. Currently two well known riffs will be used ("Smoke on the Water - Deep Purple" and "Enter Sandman – Metallica").

Before the test, please register on the system to ensure
1. you have a reliable test environment
2. you can save your current work if you want to continue later
